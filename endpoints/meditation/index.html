Select a Meditation Method

Focus on the Flame

Light a candle and watch the flame. Observe its movements and let your thoughts settle.

Focus on a Grandfather Clock

Imagine the steady ticking of a clock. Let the rhythmic sound ground you in the present.

Focus on Breathing

Close your eyes and take slow, deep breaths. Feel the air enter and leave your lungs.

Box Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 4 seconds, and repeat.

Fog Island Visualization

Imagine yourself on an island surrounded by fog. Breathe in, and the fog moves in. Breathe out, and the fog recedes.
