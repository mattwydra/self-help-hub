Social Anxiety Support

Helpful Self-Talk Phrases

When anxiety rises, try repeating these to yourself:

  • "I've handled difficult conversations before and survived."
  • "People are usually focused on themselves, not judging me."
  • "I don't need to be perfect to be accepted."
  • "This feeling will pass - it always does."
  • "My worth isn't determined by one interaction."

Social Anxiety Soundtrack

Create a confidence-boosting ritual before social situations:

  1. Create a playlist of your favorite upbeat, confidence-boosting songs.
  2. Before entering an anxiety-provoking situation, find a private space.
  3. Play one song while striking "power poses" (hands on hips, standing tall with shoulders back).
  4. As you listen, imagine yourself handling the upcoming situation with ease.

Why this works: This combines music therapy with physical posture change to boost confidence through both auditory and proprioceptive channels. Research shows that changing your physical posture can actually shift your emotional state.
